White Water Rafting Adventures

Our Mission

To provide safe, unforgettable white water rafting experiences that connect people with nature while promoting teamwork and adventure. We believe in creating memories that last a lifetime through exceptional service and expert guidance.

History

Founded in 1995 by outdoor enthusiasts Mark and Sarah Johnson, White Water Rafting Adventures began as a small family operation with just two rafts and a passion for sharing the thrill of river running.

Over the past 25+ years, we've grown into one of the region's most trusted rafting companies, maintaining our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and creating unforgettable adventures for over 50,000 satisfied customers.
